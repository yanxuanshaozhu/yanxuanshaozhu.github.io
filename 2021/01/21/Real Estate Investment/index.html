1 How to deal with townhouse noisy neighbors

1) 不买townhouse，换成sfh(single family house)

difference from google: townhouses are attached to each other, with each having at least one shared wall with adjacent townhouse, while single-family homes are freestanding

2)报告HOA(homeowner’s association), 有quiet time.如果私自把地毯换成地板不隔音, HOA会处理

3) 再装一层dry wall就可以隔音了，两层dry wall直接可以贴上high weight sound barrier，YouTube有教学

4)注意很多新建的三层th都是和邻居很近，可能有噪音
来源:https://www.1point3acres.com/bbs/thread-704123-1-1.html

3 First house in Seattle area: Real Estate vs. Stock Market – a modeling approach

It is a thorough analysis for situations in 2021, but I won’t purchase a house within the next few years, so I simply excerpt the conclusion part. The whole modeling procedure could be found from the source forum thread or the google docs.

Conclusion:

  1. In order to maximize ROE, choose least down payment and meantime lend as much as possible within the constraint of your monthly budget
  2. In order to maximize net profit, pay the most possible downpayment within your budget to obtain an low interest rate
  3. Taking both the ROE and the net profit into consideration, try to make less downpaymenet, obtain lower interest rate and lend more

Tips for those people who want an up valuation in their first house:
use full conforming loan($776250 in 2021), make a down payment between 5% ~ 20%, set a total budget between $817k ~$ 970k
