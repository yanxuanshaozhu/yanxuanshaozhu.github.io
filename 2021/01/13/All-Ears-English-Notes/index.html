Episode1234 :Example words you need to say when you are late for a plane and need to cut in line:

Excuse me, would you mind if I went in front of you?

I am so sorry to ask you this , but would it be okay if I passed you in line?

Hi, I hate to ask, but could I please cut you in line?

Reason: My plane leaves in 24 mins and I just got stuck in traffic.
